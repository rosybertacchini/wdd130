Home page

Hello, everyone! My name is Rosy Bertacchini, and I am happy to have the opportunity to introduce myself to all of you through this web site. Right now, I am a Web Developer student at BYUI (Brigham Young University of Idaho). I have experience as a System Administration and ERP implementation and support system, Additionally, I have hands-on experience in providing technical support to users, addressing their network-related concerns, and resolving issues promptly and efficiently. I bring a diverse skill set and a strong desire to make a meaningful impact in the world around me.

Throughout my career in Mexico, I have had the privilege of working on various projects that have pointed my expertise in implementing and supporting ERP system. I thrive in dynamic environments where creativity and problem-solving are valued. My approach to challenges is driven by a combination of analytical thinking and a willingness to explore innovative solutions. I firmly believe that by embracing change and continuously learning, we can unlock new possibilities and drive progress.

Beyond my professional life, I am passionate to grow food using hydroponic and improving my English, I like to expand my understanding of the American culture and what some call “Free Enterprise”. In my free time. These activities not only provide me with a well-rounded perspective but also serve as a source of inspiration and fuel my creativity. I am truly motivated with the opportunity I have to get my “Web Development” degree. I dream I will have the opportunity to collaborate with talented people, and I believe that the power of teamwork and collective intelligence is unparalleled, and I am eager to contribute my skills with my next employer.
