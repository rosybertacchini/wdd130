Projects

ERP Implementation - project 1

I had the privilege to participate in the integration of Samsung's ERP system into my current company, an experience that provided me with a comprehensive understanding of ERP systems, including their features, capabilities, and configuration options. The implementation involved significant changes to organizational processes and structures. Managing these changes effectively and working together towards a common goal certainly is the foundation of success.

Build IT rooms - project 2

In the past few years, my company has achieved significant growth, expanding our operations and establishing two new facilities. . As an integral member of the IT team, I have actively contributed to the supervision and development of two dedicated IT rooms, situated within the newly constructed buildings. This invaluable opportunity has enabled me to acquire a diverse set of skills and extensive knowledge in the field. I learned how important is as well of implementing efficient air conditioning solutions to maintain optimal temperature and humidity levels within the IT rooms. I also learned how to deploy reliable uninterruptible power supply (UPS) systems, safeguarding against potential power outages and ensuring uninterrupted operation of critical equipment. Also, I recognized the importance of physical security measures for protecting our valuable assets.
