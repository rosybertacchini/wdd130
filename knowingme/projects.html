Build IT rooms - project 2

In the past few years, my company has achieved significant growth, expanding our operations and establishing two new facilities. . As an integral member of the IT team, I have actively contributed to the supervision and development of two dedicated IT rooms, situated within the newly constructed buildings. This invaluable opportunity has enabled me to acquire a diverse set of skills and extensive knowledge in the field. I learned how important is as well of implementing efficient air conditioning solutions to maintain optimal temperature and humidity levels within the IT rooms. I also learned how to deploy reliable uninterruptible power supply (UPS) systems, safeguarding against potential power outages and ensuring uninterrupted operation of critical equipment. Another crucial aspect of this project involved establishing and implementing robust structured cabling infrastructure. Also, I recognized the importance of physical security measures for protecting our valuable assets.
